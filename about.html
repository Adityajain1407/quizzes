Quiz Portal

Quiz Portal is a personal study platform for UPSC and competitive exam preparation.

Features include:

  • Organised quiz folders by subject
  • Systematic and random quiz modes
  • Revision mode for reviewing answers
  • Google Calendar integration to display your events alongside study schedule
  • Holiday calendar for India and Madhya Pradesh

Google Calendar is used only to display your own events. No data is stored or shared.
