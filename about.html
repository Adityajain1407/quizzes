Quiz Portal

A personal study platform for UPSC and competitive exam preparation, built by Aditya Jain.

What is Quiz Portal?

Quiz Portal is a web-based study tool that helps students prepare for UPSC and other competitive examinations through organised, topic-wise quizzes. It is free to use and designed for serious aspirants.

Features

  • Topic-wise quiz folders organised by subject (History, Geography, Polity, Economy etc.)
  • Systematic mode — attempt questions in order
  • Random mode — randomised question order for better retention
  • Revision mode — review all answers with explanations
  • Save important questions for later review
  • Leaderboard to track performance
  • Calendar widget showing Indian public holidays and personal Google Calendar events

Google Calendar Integration

Quiz Portal optionally integrates with Google Calendar to display your personal events alongside Indian public holidays on the built-in calendar widget. This helps students plan their study schedule around important dates.

We only request read-only access to your calendar events. No data is ever stored, shared, or used for any purpose other than displaying events to you on your own screen.

Who can use it?

Quiz Portal is intended for students preparing for UPSC CSE, State PSC, and other competitive examinations in India. Access is by invitation from the developer.
